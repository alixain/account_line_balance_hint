Journal Line Balance Hint

Instantly view Account and Partner Balances right on the Journal Items line!

This module enhances your accounting workflow in Odoo 16 by providing an immediate, real-time hint of an account's or a partner's current balance directly when selecting them on a journal entry line.

Why choose this module?

Account Balance Hint

No more opening multiple tabs to check an account's balance! Just click or select an account in a journal item, and its current balance will be instantly displayed.

Partner Balance Hint

Keep track of what your customers owe or what you owe your vendors seamlessly. Simply hovering or selecting a partner instantly shows their outstanding balance.

Faster Data Entry

Empower your accountants to make better and faster decisions during data entry with the full context of their actions directly in the view.
